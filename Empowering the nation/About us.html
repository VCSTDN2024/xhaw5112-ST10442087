ONE STEP AT A TIME

EMPOWERING THE NATION

is a skills training initiative founded by Precious Radebe in 2018. The initiative offers six week short courses and six month learnerships that allow domestic and garden workers to up skill themselves with formal educational qualifications. The initiative makes workers marketable while seeking employment enabling them to become an asset to their employers gaining the opportunities to more skilled services by their employer. This allows them a higher pay rate due to their additional skills set moreover opening doors to entrepreneurship for their workers.
