Our six month courses.

Sewing

At the end of the course students will be expected to know how to make alterations and create their unique and original clothing from scratch. Students will be taught the different types of stitches, threading and sewing of buttons, zips, hems and seams with the use of a machine.

Fees: R1500

Purpose: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services

Content:

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Landscaping

Students will be taught to provide landscaping services to new and established gardens. Their skills will be tailored to indigenous and exotic plants and trees as well as balancing of those garden plants. Students will be able to install fixed structures such as fountains, statues, benches, tables and built-in-braai stands. They will be able to identify not only aesthetics of plant shapes and colours but will also learn the types of garden layouts.

Fees: R1500

Purpose: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens

Content:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

First Aid

The course aims to provide students with basic life support and first aid awareness by showing students how to tend to wounds, burns, fractures and bleeding. CPR will also be covered for situations where someone is choking or has a blocked airway.

Fees: R1500

Purpose: To provide first aid awareness and basic life support

Content:

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress e.g., Choking, blocked airway

Life Skills

Students will be expected to navigate basic necessities such as opening bank accounts, learn basic labour laws, reading and writing literacy and basic numeric literacy.

Fees: R1500

Purpose: To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities

Content:

  • Opening a bank account
  • Basic labour law (know your rights)
  • Basic reading and writing literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy
